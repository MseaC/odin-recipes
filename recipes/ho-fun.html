Beef Ho Fun

Description

A super quick tasty meal, perfect topped with a dollop of crispy chilli

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 270g sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 20g oyster sauce
  • 4 tsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 110g flat rice noodles
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 onion, finely sliced
  • 90g beansprouts
  • 160g pak choi, leaves separated
  1. Mix together the oyster sauce, soy sauce, and cornflour in a blow. Add the beef and marinate for 10-15 minutes
  2. Cook the noodles according to packet instructions and set aside
  3. Heat half the oil in a wok over high heat, add the onions and fry for 2-3 minutes until softened
  4. Remove the onions form the wok and set aside
  5. Add remaining oil and fry the beef for 2-3 minutes
  6. Return the onions to the wok and add the noodles. Fry for a minute then add the pok choi, beansprouts and a splash of water
  7. Cook for a minute then tip into a bowl, top with crunchy chilli, and eat immediately
