Prawn and Chorizo Stir-Fry

Description

A quick and tasty mid-week meal

Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 80g wholewheat noodles
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 110g chorizo, sliced/li>
  • 220g prawns
  • 1 red onion, sliced
    • ssss
  • 1 green pepper, sliced
  • 1 large courgette, sliced
  • 2 red chilli, sliced
  • 1 bunch spring onions
  • 2 tbsp sriracha
  • Small bunch coriander

Method

  1. Cook noodles according to packet instructions
  2. Melt coconut oil in a large wok or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Fry the chorizo for 3-4 minutes until crisp. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on kitchen roll
  4. Increase the heat, add the prawns and stir-fry for a couple of minutes until they turn pink. Remove from the pan and set aside
  5. Add the onion, pepper, courgette, and chilli, and fry for 3-4 minutes until soft
  6. Add the spring onions, stir-fry for a minute, then add cooked noodles and chilli sauce
  7. Return the chorizo and prawns to the pan, toss to coat, then season to taste
  8. Scatter over coriander and serve.
